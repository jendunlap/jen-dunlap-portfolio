HOGWARTS ROLLCALL

BELIEVE IN MAGIC

Welcome to Hogwarts! As part of a three day hackathon we created a magical index of Hogwarts students, their houses and their classes. Dive in to full CRUD capabilities on front and backend to create your own student or house, and explore the magical world of Hogwarts!

TECHNOLOGY USED

PostreSQL | Express | React.js | Node.js

FEATURES

Integration and navigation between two APIs (both with create, read, update and delete capabilities), along with really beautiful conditional rendering based on each student's Hogwarts house.
