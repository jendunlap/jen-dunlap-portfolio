TECHNOLOGY USED

MongoDB | Express | React.js | Node.js

FEATURES

New friends made in a 48 hour hackathon, lots of jokes, some terrible puns, and custom-ish graphics created by myself, based on Roller Coaster Tycoon (and chock full of corn!)
