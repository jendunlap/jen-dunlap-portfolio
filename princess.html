Princess Generator

EVERYONE'S A PRINCESS!

GUEST LOGIN: guest@guest.com PASSWORD: guest

Let's go girls (and boys and non-binary royalty)! Everyone's a princess with the Princess Generator! Model one after yourself, your friends, or create someone new!

TECHNOLOGY USED

MongoDB | Express | React.js | Node.js

FEATURES

Front and backend user authentication, full front and backend CRUD (create, read, update and delete) capabilities, and some fairly intense conditional rendering, switch cases and more to create the functionality of the generator. All images are hand drawn (by me, Jen Dunlap!) with loads of combinations to choose from, including hairstyles, color, eye shape, skintone and more!
